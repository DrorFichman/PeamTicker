## SEO Tips for Play Store

1. **Use keywords naturally** - Don't keyword stuff
2. **Front-load important terms** - Put key phrases early in descriptions
3. **Include numbers and specifics** - "1000+ users" builds credibility
4. **Use formatting** - Bullets and sections improve readability
5. **Update regularly** - Fresh content signals active development
6. **Encourage reviews** - Higher ratings = better visibility
7. **Respond to reviews** - Shows active support
8. **Use all character limits** - Maximize available space
9. **Include call-to-action** - Tell users what to do
10. **Highlight unique features** - WhatsApp integration, AI, chemistry tracking
